# Competitive intelligence

## Purpose

Produce structured, source-grounded intelligence on competitor companies —
financial position, product moves, and public positioning — for use in
strategy and planning. Every finding traces to a cited public source.

## Workflows available

- **company-research** — Given a target company, gather public sources,
  extract key metrics, and produce a one-page competitor summary.

## What this workspace does NOT do

- No private, paywalled, or non-public data sources.
- No forward-looking financial projections or investment advice.
- No outreach, contact, or any action directed at the competitor.
- No analysis of the host organisation itself — competitors only.

## Shared constraints

- Every claim in an artifact must cite a public source (URL + access date).
- Quote figures as reported; never estimate or interpolate missing numbers.
- If a required source cannot be found, record the gap — do not substitute.
- Currency is USD unless the source states otherwise.
